About me

I grew up in the small town of Mount Pleasant, Utah. My love for computers started during my childhood when I played the videogame "Putt-Putt Goes to the Moon". The virtual world has always been intriguing to me. During my free time, I enjoy hiking, rock climbing, music and spending time with my close friends.

I am a student at Utah Valley University studying Computer Science with an emphasis in Full-Stack Development. I am in the Honors Program and currently hold a 3.9 GPA. I am on track to graduate May 2021.

I have worked as a full-stack developer, website designer and technical support at Etrafficers, Inc. for almost three years. During my time at Etrafficers, I have developed, deployed and maintained over 30 mortgage website templates and 75 mortgage company websites (See some examples). I worked closely with clients customizing their websites to fit their needs. I have built a new dashboard for loan officers to view loan applications. I also have built a website editor for clients to make their own changes to their websites.
